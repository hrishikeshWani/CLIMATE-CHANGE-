Climate Change Awareness

Climate change is causing rising temperatures worldwide.
Melting glaciers contribute to rising sea levels.
Extreme weather events are becoming more frequent.
Human activities are the primary cause of global warming.
Deforestation increases CO₂ levels, worsening climate change.

How Climate Change Affects Us

Heat Waves

Climate change increases the frequency of dangerous heat waves affecting health and agriculture.

Flooding

Rising sea levels and extreme rainfall increase the risk of severe floods worldwide.

Food Shortages

Crop failures become more common due to unpredictable weather patterns.

What We Can Do Daily

Save Electricity

Turn off unused lights, appliances, and switch to energy-efficient LEDs.

Use Reusable Products

Reduce single-use plastics by using reusable bottles and bags.

Plant Trees

Planting trees helps absorb CO₂ and cool the environment naturally.
